Roadmap 2022

As stated in our vision we want to be as transparent as possible. This also means showing which features we are working on. Since Beanconqueror is still our hobby project, we can't give definite dates but show the topics below. So check them out and give us feedback or enter your feature wishes on Github.

1

QR Scan

We want to make entering a new bean easier for you and interesting for the roaster. For that specific case, we are working on a QR-code scanning system which enters each bean details in the app - with ALL infos the roaster will give us.

We are happy to announce that we already got some cooperations!

2

Transparency

Since the app and its open source approach always focuses on transparency, we want to continue to extend this and show running costs, usage of tracking data etc. We will provide all information on the following detail pages:
Running costs
Data & tracking

3

Integration of more Bluetooth Scales

For now we already support the Decent Scale aswell as the older Acaia Lunar scales. We want to support more Acaia, Feliciata and the Skale 2, bluetooth scales

4

Cost Covering

Maybe its the year of cost covering. Beanconqueror is free and open source. But costs are still there which is approximated 300€ per year
