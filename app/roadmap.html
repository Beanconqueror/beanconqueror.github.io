Roadmap 2021

As stated in our vision we want to be as transparent as possible. This also means showing which features we are working on. Since Beanconqueror is still our hobby project, we can't give definite dates but show the topics below. So check them out and give us feedback or enter your feature wishes in Github.

1

Home Roasting

We want to support home roasters with advanced features to enter your beans, roasting details and then reuse the beans for each brew.

2

QR Scan

We want to make entering a new bean easier for you and interesting for the roaster. For that specific case, we are working on a QR-code scanning system which enters each bean details in the app - with ALL infos the roaster will give us.

We are happy to anounce already here that we got some cooperations together

3

New Tracking

As stated in our vision, we want to move to 100% Germany-based data ownership and a transparent tracking concept. For that, we want to switch from Google Analytics to a service, which WILL NOT sell your data. So we will change our current tracking concept. All details will be documented here.

4

Transparency

Since the app and its open source approach always focuses on transparency, we want to continue extend this and show running costs, usage of tracking data etc. For that we will enter information on the following detail pages:
